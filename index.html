The Tic Tac Toe app does not collect, store, process, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

2. Offline Gameplay

The app operates entirely offline. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

3. No Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party services such as analytics tools, advertising networks, social logins, or SDKs that collect user data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Since the app collects no personal data, it is safe for use by children of all ages and complies with COPPA and other child privacy regulations.

5. Data Security

As we do not collect or store personal data, there is no risk of data misuse or unauthorized access.
